<section class="lang-en">
	<h1>Questions & Answers</h1>
	<h2>Why is Linux better than Windows or macOS?</h2>
	<p>If you are not familiar with it, Linux is an operating system, the software that controls computer hardware and allows other programs to run. Linux is often considered better than Windows or macOS for several reasons. Here are a few:</p>
	<p>First, Linux is generally more stable and secure than Windows or macOS. It is less susceptible to viruses and malware, and it is usually less likely to crash or become unstable. That makes it a strong option for people who care about privacy and reliability.</p>
	<p>Second, Linux is often more customizable. Users can modify and tune the system to match their needs and keep strong control over how it behaves.</p>
	<p>Third, Linux is usually free or very low cost. Many distributions are open source, which means anyone can download and use them at no cost.</p>
	<p>Overall, Linux is a powerful and versatile operating system with meaningful advantages. It is stable, secure, customizable, and often free, which makes it a great choice for many types of users.</p>
</section>
